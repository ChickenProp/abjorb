How to play: Click on the screen to propell yourself in the opposite diections, using part of yourself to create the force. To win you must become the largest orb. If you collide with other orbs, the larger one will ABJORB the smaller one. If antimatter collides with normal matter then they will destroy each other.
  • Green orb: the player.
  • Blue orb: matter that is smaller than the player.
  • Red orb: matter that is larger than the player.
  • Orange orb: antimatter that is smaller than the player.
  • Purple orb: antimatter that is larger than the player.
